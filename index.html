About

Ambition Bistro and Coffee Bar

We're a funky urban bistro that takes great pride in offering fresh foods. Whether it's coffee, a sandwich, dessert, or a beverage from our full bar, we're going to make sure you enjoy it and that you'll want to come back. We've just become nationally recognized with our book, Is The Coffee Fresh? written by Ambition owner, Marc Renson. Find it at amazon.com or ask for it at any major bookhouse. Ambition is truly a hidden gem in Schenectady (and celebrities who come in will never forget us!). Take a look at our wonderful menu and our calendar of events and you'll find everything that you need! Don't forget to "like us" on facebook and Follow us on Twitter @Ambitioncafe We hope to see you soon!

Is The Coffee Fresh?

People are Crazy! And author/owner Marc Renson knows it best! After twelve years of owning Ambition Coffee & Eatery, Marc has seen and heard just about everything. Now he has penned his first book about all the dysfunction in the food service industry, both front and back of the house. Read about his more questionable customers what they do and say while visiting Ambition that keeps Marc and his staff on their toes wondering what are they gonna do next! Buy Is The Coffee Fresh? at Ambition, at any bookhouse or on line at amazon.com!
